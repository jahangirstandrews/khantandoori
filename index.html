Khan Tandoori is Dundee’s most established and longest running Indian takeaway.

A family run business specialising in the finest Indian cuisine, as well as a whole variety of other foods. We cater with a traditional delicacy to provide the highest quality of food for all of our customers.

You can order for a quick pick up or a delivery to anywhere in Dundee or a number of areas out-with the city. Our menu ranges from but isn’t limited to curries, kebabs, pizzas and many more. You can view the menu from the site and choose from your favourite dishes and foods.
